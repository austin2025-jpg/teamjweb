About Us

About TeamJesus Youth Organization

TeamJesus Youth Organization is a Christ-centered movement established on December 31, 2020 with a divine calling: "Winning the lost at all costs." We inspire, equip, and mobilize young people to live out the Gospel through discipleship, outreach, and transformation.

Founder: Godfrey H. Monbo

In obedience to a divine vision, Godfrey H. Monbo founded TeamJesus to transform young lives through the power of Christ. His leadership is marked by compassion, clarity of purpose, and a deep desire to raise spiritual and social changemakers.

Aims & Objectives

  • Win souls for Christ
  • Mentor and educate the youth
  • Support the vulnerable and marginalized
  • Promote God-given talents
  • Advocate against rape and drug abuse
  • Restore broken lives spiritually and socially

Our Core Activities

  • Fasting & Prayer Retreats
  • Youth Tarry Nights
  • Seminars & Empowerment Events
  • Community Evangelism
  • Charity & Social Impact Drives
  • Youth Sports & Talent Competitions
  • Bible Quizzing Programs
